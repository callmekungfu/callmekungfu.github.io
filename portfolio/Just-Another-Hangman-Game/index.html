Unionville Highschool Athletic Rebranding

Project includes:
LOGO DESIGN, GRAPHICS DESIGN, PRODUCT DESIGN

Unionville High School is a dynamic and supportive learning community that fosters the skills and attitudes necessary for critical thinking, lifelong inquiry and learning, and active and engaged citizenship. The main purpose of this project is to create a graphical emblem for the client's new physical education uniform as well as other clothing products what will be worn by thousands of students and inspire the next designer authoring the emblem. The emblem is created based on tens of drafts and sketches. Evolved and changed dynamically according to the client's requests.

THAT'S ALL THERE IS TO THIS PROJECT.
