Spike It Volleyball Summer Camp Rebranding

Project includes:
LOGO DESIGN, GRAPHICS DESIGN, WEB DESIGN, WEB DEVELOPMENT, SOLUTION PRODUCTION, WEB DEVELOPEMENT

Ran by Ontario volleyball coach Raymond Kong, Spike It Volleyball Camp is a private camp that foucuses on the development of volleyball skills with emphasis on technique, fun and team play. This brand is a great representation of both elegance and strength, providing a branding solution is both complex and interesting.

When I contacted Yonglin with the possibility of creating a website, I had lots of ideas and no clue how to make them all come together. - Camp Director Ray Kong

The result is a website which is engaging, informative, interesting and a valuable representation of my summer camp. I have received a great deal of feedback from registrants. His brilliant design makes registration efficient, information collection and organizing simple, and promotion of the camp is ideal. - Camp Director Ray Kong

The primary purpose of this project is to create a website that serves as an information display and a registration portal, as well as a new online registration system that reduces paperwork and increases registration efficiency for the camp. You can visit the website via spikeit.ca.

The secondary purpose is to refine the camp's previous logo, to improve the drawing quality, polish the font choice and cultivate the layout of the graphics, to define the true elegance and power in this brand. In English, I needed to make the logo prettier.

Yonglin's willingness to revise and amend the site ensured terrific results.
