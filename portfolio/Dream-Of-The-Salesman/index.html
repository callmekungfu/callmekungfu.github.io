Dream of the Salesman - an English Documentary Project

Project includes:
SCREEN WRITING, FILM MAKING, PHOTOGRAPHY, EDITING, SOUND DESIGN

Contributors:
Michael Kwan, Ryan Huang, Jennifer Chan

For our Grade 11 English Film Project, we decided to create a documentary based on Arthur Miller's 1949 play - Death of a Salesman. The course of principal photography took over 7 days, with editing taking nearly 15 hours.

The film documented Biff Loman's thoughts and action when he was looking back at his father Willy Loman's personalities and their relationships. The documentary is set in the 1980s, where Biff is around the same age Willy is when he suicided.

THAT'S ALL THERE IS TO THIS PROJECT.
