For Students

Uhs.life is built by students, for students.

Anytime Anywhere

Announcements on Uhs.life is always avaliable, no need to stress about missing the PA.

Get Updated

Weekly bulletin, mobile notification, You can count on our tailored notifications to keep you in touch.

Search, Discover

Realtime Search Engine Powered by Algolia, find club, team, course information in the blink of an eye.

You Mean Something To Us

Dedicated feedback section and support email for students, we are always listening to you.

For Teachers & Club Leaders

Uhs.life makes engaging with the students a breeze.

Dynamic Editor

Introducing "The Composer", an editor that allows you to publish content with personalities, whether it's through a poster, a video, or just a line of text. The choice is yours.

Go Beyond Announcements

Publish Blogs. Tell a tale, change a life. Share some knowledge, build a community.

Streamlined Experience

You can view the review status of all announcement requests, and the reson they are rejected.

It Keeps Getting Better

With new updates coming out monthly, look forward to be able to share gallaries, videos, google drive files and so much more.

For Administrators

Uhs.life provides a simple and secure information management experience.

Fully Customizable

From categories to clubs, user privleges and platform access rule. Everything can be customized and tailored to the needs of the school.

Synchronize Everywhere

If the school has a Twitter account, other social media accounts, or traditional TV kiosks, Uhs.life can synchronize content from to them.

Privacy & Security

Security is one of our top priorities, we work hard to protect the privacy of school and students. For more information, read our privacy policy.

Automate Tasks

Sit back, relax. Uhs.life provides tools to simplify the process of information management, including automated bulletin and automated announcement publishing.
