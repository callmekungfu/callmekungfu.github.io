Experiences

Humble yet mighty.

  • Late 2015

    Every end is a new beginning.

    No one likes doing a minimum-wage job, neither did I. After half a year of "diligent" working at a Druxy's restaurant, I decided to call a quit on this physically and emotionally demainding job. I did so that I can have more time persuing my interests and to improve my academics.

  • End of 2015

    The first step is always difficult.

    One day, I saw a poster about school clothing design contest, I decided to take place in the challenge. So I created a "low poly wolf" from reference stock photos to represent the mascot of our school - a wolf. Regarding support, this project was a success, voted to be the top 2 designs by the students of our school. However, the design was declined due to its complexity. From this experience I learned that it is crucial to keep the designs simple.
